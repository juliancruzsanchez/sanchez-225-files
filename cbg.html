CBG Benefits

CBG is a is a non-psychoactive compound found in the cannabis plant, meaning that it does not produce the "high" effects associated with THC usage. It is often taken together with CBD to help with pain and inflammation. Unlike CBD, CBG only appears in young cannabis plants.

What are the benefits of CBG?

It has been found that CBG can have a variety of benefits. It has been found to help things such as pain, inflammation, anxiety, and more.

How does CBG work?

Similarly to CBD, CBG works by interacting with the endocannabinoid system. The endocannabinoid system is one of the systems in the body that regulates things like pain, inflammation, and anxiety. It also, unlike CBD, has been found to help with an array of different disorders. These include but are not limited to:

  • Huntington’s disease
  • Parkinson’s disease
  • multiple sclerosis
  • inflammatory bowel disease (IBD)

How much CBG should I take?

Consult with yoir doctor before taking CBG. They can help you determine the right dose for your body.

What are the side effects of CBG?

While there are no known side effects of CBG, it is important to talk with a doctor to ensure it won't interact with any medications you may be taking.

What are the potential risks of CBG?

There are not known risks for taking CBG. Nobody has ever died from taking CBG, CBD, or THC. Once again make sure to talk with your doctor before taking CBG.
